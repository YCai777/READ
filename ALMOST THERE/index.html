Video Meeting

Video conferencing does restore some essential nonverbal elements: participants can see each other's facial expressions and hear intonation and tone of voice.Video conferencing does restore some essential nonverbal elements: participants can see each other's facial expressions and hear intonation and tone of voice. This makes conveying and interpreting emotions much easier than via email or chat. As a result, video calls rank high on the richness scale of communication media because they can convey multiple cues at once. However, video communication also highlights nonverbal gaps. Video is limited to what the camera captures — usually the head and shoulders. Important information such as full body posture and interpersonal distance is lost. Due to the position of the camera, direct eye contact is impossible: a person's gaze cannot be focused on the screen and the camera simultaneously, so it always appears to drift away from the other person. In group videoconferencing, peripheral attention has nowhere to go across a grid of small rectangles. Additionally, latency turns immediacy into a random signal, disrupting the natural rhythm of reaction. For participants, the effort required to read and send nonverbal signals creates fatigue — video communication demands constant concentration in ways that a shared physical room does not.

Discord

In Discord, people don't just stay in the channel and communicate, or in this case, communication exists as a medium, where friends can watch movies and enjoy music together, or go on adventures in virtual bars or deep sea and high skies in games.

Communication also becomes a medium for friends to watch movies and enjoy music together, or to go exploring in a virtual bar or deep sea skies in a game to increase interaction. However, these avatars are often unable to accurately convey real emotions and are often based on another medium, which can change moods for better or worse.

All of this distances people from each other and makes friends more like teammates than close friends.

Emojis, GIFs, Reaction Buttons, Typing Indicators, Read Receipts

Emojis function as digital facial expressions. GIFs simulate gestures or tone. Typing dots signal anticipation. Read receipts confirm acknowledgment.

These elements attempt to replicate social presence. They provide micro-signals that suggest I am here.

But they remain abstractions.
